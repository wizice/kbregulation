1.3.2. 부록 1. 침습적 시술 목록 담당부서 의료질 향상 및 환자안전위원회 제정일 2012.05. 최종개정일 2021.01. 최종검토일 2025.03.

침습적 시술 목록 (Invasive procedure list)

1) Cardiac procedures (e.g., cardiac catheterization, angioplasty, stent implantation)

2) Invasive radiology procedures (e.g., angiography, angioplasty, line insertion)

3) Endoscopy (e.g., endoscopy, colonoscopy, ERCP, bronchoscopy, TEE, cystoscopy)

4) Percutaneous aspiration of body fluids (e.g., thoracentesis, paracentesis, chest tube insertion)

5) Central line insertion

6) Biopsy that put patients at more than minimal risk

7) Invasive ophthalmic procedure (Conjunctival mass excision)

8) Dermatology procedures (e.g., flap, skin graft, liposuction)

9) Otorhinolaryngology procedures (e.g., V-tube insertion)

10) Lumbar puncture (e.g., Spinal tap) *ERCP: Endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography *TEE: Transesophageal Echocardiography
